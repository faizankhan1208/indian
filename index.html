About the School

Established in 2006 Indian Academy, through its continued dedication in pioneering education and maintaining school traditions, has earned its reputation as a sincere school. It is an unaided minority institution. The school offers students facilities such as huge sports ground for various sports played along with educational programs in pattern with the Indian school certificate education board (ISCE & ISC) based in New Delhi. Over 400 Students are enrolled proudly with the school. We believe in a good student: Teacher ratio being important to the growth of each teacher &student under our using. The school looks forward to a bright future.

Indian Academy aims at rendering quality education and offers matchless opportunities in the fields of academics, games, curricular and co-curricular activities. We want each child grow in all aspects of life and not just basic education. We believe in education beyond books. Children who pass out from our school can be proudly claimed as all–rounders, with value based education and every quality essential for taking on life’s challenges with confidence and conviction.The increasing demand for admissions and classes has resulted in the addition of new classrooms, an extension of the library and computer labs. Smart classes are also introduced from this academic session.
