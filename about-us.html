About the School

Established in 2006 Indian Academy, through its continued dedication in pioneering education and maintaining school traditions, has earned its reputation as a sincere school. It is an unaided minority institution. The school offers students facilities such as huge sports ground for various sports played along with educational programs in pattern with the Indian school certificate education board (ISCE & ISC) based in New Delhi. Over 400 Students are enrolled proudly with the school. We believe in a good student: Teacher ratio being important to the growth of each teacher &student under our using. The school looks forward to a bright future. Indian Academy aims at rendering quality education and offers matchless opportunities in the fields of academics, games, curricular and co-curricular activities. We want each child grow in all aspects of life and not just basic education. We believe in education beyond books. Children who pass out from our school can be proudly claimed as all–rounders, with value based education and every quality essential for taking on life’s challenges with confidence and conviction. The increasing demand for admissions and classes has resulted in the addition of new classrooms, an extension of the library and computer labs. Smart classes are also introduced from this academic session.

The school was founded by a great educationist by himself, (Late) Dr. Matin Khan (PhD. Marketing, MBA and Engineering from IIT Roorekee). He retired as the H.O.D. (head of department) for MBA in Lucknow University. Opening up a school and making education available for one and all had been on his mind for many years. Having belonged to the educational line he was very fond of children and hence laid the foundation of the school, today known as Indian Academy. His sudden demise in 2008 soon after the school was founded left us hollow , however his dream and love for children both boys and girls is being taken forward by his family and friends. They try hard to do the best in their ability. To live up to his expectations. Hiring quality staff to be able to impart education to every child cutting the premises of Indian academy is our main goal.
